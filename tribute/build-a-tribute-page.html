Monroe Dunaway Anderson

From cotton to cancer

Monroe Dunaway Anderson, or M.D. Anderson, was a cotton magnate and philanthropist who is resposible for the creation of the M.D. Anderson Foundation, which is the leading organization of the the M.D. Anderson Cancer Center, one of the leading cancer treatment centers in the United States.
